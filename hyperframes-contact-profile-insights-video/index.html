What it builds
From URLs to profiles

AiFetchly visits URLs, scans pages, follows useful links, and deduplicates results.

With AI Enrichment enabled, each profile can include phone numbers, addresses, and social links when the public page content supports it.

1. Website URLs
Manual list or search result URLs.
2. Page review
Emails, internal links, and useful candidate pages.
3. Contact profile
Emails plus optional phone, address, social links, and AI status.
