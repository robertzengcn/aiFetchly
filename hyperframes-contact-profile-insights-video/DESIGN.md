## Style Prompt

Create a clean product-training video for AiFetchly's Contact Profile Insights feature. The look should echo the live app: a bright white workspace, soft gray navigation panels, dark readable labels, periwinkle primary actions, and green success/status chips. The composition should feel calm, instructional, and operational, with product UI mockups that are dense enough to be credible but never crowded.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#F7F8FC`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Surface muted: `#EEF0F5`
- Text primary: `#23242A`
- Text secondary: `#6E7480`
- Primary action: `#6366F1`
- Primary action soft: `#C7C9FF`
- Success/status: `#DDF7E6`
- Success text: `#2EA568`
- Warning/accent: `#F4B942`

## Typography

- Primary UI/font stack: `Inter`, `Helvetica Neue`, `Arial`, sans-serif, matching the existing AiFetchly tutorial videos.
- Use bold, compact headings and medium-weight interface labels.
- Keep captions, table labels, and form fields large enough for video legibility at 1080p.

## Motion Rules

- Use short, confident UI motions: 0.35-0.7s entrances, small vertical offsets, slight scale for callouts.
- Highlight steps with soft focus rings, cursor dots, and panel slides.
- Use visible white wipe transitions between scenes.
- Avoid frantic transitions; this is a tutorial for users following along.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use dark cyber/neon styling.
- Do not use generic stock-like visuals disconnected from the app.
- Do not crowd the frame with every manual detail at once.
- Do not use gradient text or decorative blobs.
- Do not make the training feel like a marketing landing page.
